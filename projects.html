Our Projects

Nirantara Foundation is committed to community upliftment through impactful projects in education, health, and empowerment. We provide essential resources, conduct workshops, and support underprivileged individuals to foster sustainable development. Together, we aim to create lasting change for a better future.

Arpana

Giving to Society, Donating to the Needy. Arpana focuses on the spirit of giving by organizing donation drives and supporting underprivileged communities.

MyDream

Education Through Short Films, Higher Education, and Career Guidance. MyDream is dedicated to improving educational opportunities for students.

Shikshana

Educating the Needy and Sponsoring Orphans' Education. Shikshana supports educational empowerment by funding the education of underprivileged children.
